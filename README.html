The waycast, a form of way declaration

Here is project wayic.cast. This project documents the form of way declaration known as the waycast. The waycast is a basic component of the wayic architecture, where it functions as the principal building block and propagule of the wayic in service to its purpose as a self-instituting system of aimed way formation.
